14.2. Recreate Show desktop Icon

  1. Create "Show desktop.scf"
  2. Edit [Shell] Command=2 IconFile=explorer.exe,3 [Taskbar] Command=ToggleDesktop
  3. Drag the Show desktop icon to your Quick Launch toolbar.
